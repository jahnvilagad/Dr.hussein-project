Services

Examination

Regular dental examinations are the foundation of good oral health. Our proficient team conducts thorough check-ups, using the latest technology to identify and address any dental issues early on. This ensures that your smile remains healthy and beautiful. During an examination, our dentists will review your medical and dental history, check for cavities and gum disease, take X-rays, and discuss oral hygiene habits, offering recommendations for improvement.

Learn More

Scale & Polish

Professional dental cleaning is essential for maintaining healthy teeth and gums. Our scale and polish service remove plaque and tartar buildup, leaving your teeth smooth and sparkling. This preventive measure helps avoid more serious dental problems in the future. The procedure includes the use of special tools to remove staining from tea, coffee, and smoking, making your bonding look like new.

Learn More

Invisalign

Achieve the straight smile you’ve always wanted with Invisalign, a discreet and effective orthodontic treatment. Our Invisalign experts create customized treatment plans using clear aligners that are virtually invisible. Enjoy the convenience and confidence of a straighter smile without traditional braces. Invisalign can address crowded teeth, overbite, underbite, crossbite, and open bite.

Learn More

Teeth Whitening

Transform your smile with our teeth whitening services. Our dental professionals use safe and effective methods to brighten your teeth, reducing stains and discolouration. Leave our clinic with a radiant and confident smile. Teeth whitening is suitable for discoloured teeth caused by ageing, stains from food, drinks, or tobacco use, and teeth darkened by root canal treatment.

Learn More

Composite Bonding

Experience a versatile cosmetic dental solution with composite bonding. Our skilled dentists use tooth-coloured resin to repair chipped, cracked, or discoloured teeth, providing a natural and beautiful appearance. Composite bonding can also be used to increase the length of short teeth, close gaps, and improve the overall appearance of misshapen teeth.

Learn More

Veneers

Dental veneers are thin, customized shells covering the front surface of your teeth, offering an excellent solution for correcting imperfections like stains, gaps, or misaligned teeth. Our team collaborates with you to design and apply veneers that enhance your smile’s aesthetics.

Learn More
